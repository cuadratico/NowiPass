<i> NowiPass </i> is a secure password manager that uses AES to encrypt your passwords with high security.

<b> Additional features: </b>

- <i> NowiPass </i> does not have Internet access.
- <i> NowiPass </i> stores your session logs so you always know who is logged in.
- <i> NowiPass </i> is a manager where only you can manage your passwords.
